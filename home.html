Company Overview

私たちの事業内容

At Armin’s Specifications, we specialize in high-performance tuning and rare vehicle sourcing. Whether it’s restoring a classic JDM icon or pushing modern builds to their limits, we deliver precision-crafted machines built for legacy. Our passion drives everything — from ECU remaps and widebody kits to curated car sales for true enthusiasts.

We don’t just modify cars — we elevate them. Every build is engineered with intention, blending modern performance upgrades with timeless style. Our workshop is a space where innovation meets tradition, and where every detail, from suspension geometry to intake acoustics, is fine-tuned for peak driving emotion.

For us, it’s not about mass production — it’s about meaningful connection. We work closely with each client to understand their vision, their story, and the kind of machine that truly speaks to them. Because at the end of the day, we don’t just sell cars. We build legends.
