About Armin’s Specifications

Company Overview

Armin’s Specifications is a premier automotive modification company fueled by a passion for JDM performance and culture. We provide expert craftsmanship in:

  • Advanced engine tuning solutions
  • Custom widebody conversions
  • Precision exhaust fabrication
  • Suspension upgrades and enhancements
  • Sourcing and selling authentic, stock JDM vehicles

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower automotive enthusiasts by transforming their visions into reality. Through performance tuning and style-focused upgrades, we deliver customized JDM experiences while maintaining authenticity and reliability. From sourcing rare imports to fully custom builds, our goal is excellence in every detail.
